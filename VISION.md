# Pixie Vision

## Our Mission

To democratize access to blockchain technology by making wallet interactions as simple as having a conversation.

## The Problem

Traditional crypto wallets are complex and intimidating for newcomers. Users must:
- Understand technical jargon
- Navigate complex interfaces
- Manually construct transactions
- Worry about security risks

## Our Solution

Pixie transforms wallet interaction through natural language processing. Simply chat with your wallet:

```
"Send 5 SOL to my friend"
"What's my balance?"
"Swap 10 USDC for SOL"
```

## Core Values
